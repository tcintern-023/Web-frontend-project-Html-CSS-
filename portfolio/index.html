ABOUT ME

A developer who cares about the details.

I'm a Computer Science student with a soft spot for the front end, the layer people actually touch. I like taking a rough idea and shaping it into something that loads fast, reads clearly, and works on every screen.

Right now I'm going deep on the fundamentals: semantic HTML, modern CSS layout with Flexbox and Grid, and responsive design that doesn't fall apart on a phone. I believe you can't fake a strong foundation, so I write everything by hand before reaching for a framework.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me breaking down websites I admire to figure out how they were built.
